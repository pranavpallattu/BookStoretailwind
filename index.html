Featured Authors

Arundhati Roy

Arundhati Roy is an Indian author, best known for her Booker Prize-winning novel The God of Small Things (1997). Her work explores themes of politics, social justice, and history. Apart from fiction, she is also a passionate activist, writing extensively on issues like environmentalism, nuclear policy, and human rights.

John Green

John Green is an American author known for his young adult novels, including The Fault in Our Stars and Looking for Alaska. His works often explore themes of love, loss, and self-discovery. Green is also a prominent YouTube creator and philanthropist, co-founding the channel VlogBrothers.

Shashi Tharoor

Shashi Tharoor is an Indian politician, diplomat, and author. A former United Nations official, he has served as a Member of Parliament and is known for his eloquent speeches. Tharoor has written numerous books, including The Elephant, the Tiger, and the Cell Phone, exploring India's politics, history, and culture.

Robin Sharma

Robin Sharma is a Canadian author, speaker, and leadership expert, best known for his best-selling books The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari and The 5 AM Club. His works focus on personal development, leadership, and productivity, inspiring individuals to achieve success through discipline, self-mastery, and positive habits.
